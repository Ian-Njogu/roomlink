Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my property?

Simply create an account, click "Post a Listing", and fill in your property details. Our team will review and approve your listing within 24 hours.

Is it free to use RoomLink Nairobi?

Yes! Browsing and searching for properties is completely free. Landlords pay a small fee when their property is successfully rented.

How do I contact a landlord?

Once you find a property you like, you can contact the landlord directly through the phone number or email provided in the listing.

Are all landlords verified?

We verify all landlords and their properties to ensure the safety and reliability of our platform for our users.
